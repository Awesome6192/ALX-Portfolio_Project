About

FanFaction was born out of a deep passion for football and the desire to create a space where fans can freely share their thoughts, ideas, and support for their favorite clubs. Our goal is to build a thriving community where everyone has a voice. This project began as a small idea in 2023 when I wanted a platform to discuss the highs and lows of my favorite club. Over time, it evolved into a full-fledged web application. This is a Portfolio Project for ALX Africa.
